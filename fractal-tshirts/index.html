Fractal T-shirts

Math-themed shirt design for my classmates

May 30, 2014

I designed and printed t-shirts for my math class at LCC. The letters are fractals: the L is a golden dragon and the C’s are Lévy C curves. I drew them with recursive JavaScript functions on an HTML5 2D canvas.

We wore the shirts on our allegedly educational trip to the amusement park Canada’s Wonderland.
